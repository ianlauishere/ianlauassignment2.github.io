Features of SAFRA Jurong EnergyOne Gym

Located near Boon Lay MRT, the EnergyOne Gym at Jurong SAFRA is a large two-story gym with a total area of 12,000-sqft. On the first floor, you will find a wide variety of cardio equipment and resistance training machines.

Level 2 offers a free weights training area featuring a whole series of Hammer Strength equipment! A spacious fitness studio is also located on level 2, which is perfect for small group fitness classess!
